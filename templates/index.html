About

My name is Irmelin Shively, I was born in Czechoslovakia when the sun was in Libra, the moon in Leo, and Cancer on the horizon. I have lived a life full of joy and love, but also tragedy and hardship. I wish to leave art as my legacy, and share the beauty I see in the world around me. The human spirit is amazing, this is a fact I will always cherish.
